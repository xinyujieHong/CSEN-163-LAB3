What is EcoTrading?

EcoTrading is a sustainable waste management platform designed to promote the circular economy by enabling individuals and communities to exchange, repurpose, and recycle reusable items. Instead of discarding waste, users can list items they no longer need such as books, jars, clothing, electronics, or plastic materials and trade them with others who may find them useful.
The platform not only facilitates the exchange of goods but also introduces a reward-based system, where users can earn points for trading recyclable materials like paper, metal, and plastic. These points can be redeemed for various benefits, encouraging active participation in reducing environmental impact.
Beyond trading, EcoTrading serves as an educational hub, offering resources on upcycling, waste sorting, and sustainable living practices. It also features a sustainability tracker, allowing users to measure their personal and collective impact on reducing landfill waste and CO‚ÇÇ emissions.
By connecting people through responsible waste management, EcoTrading transforms waste into value, reduces landfill waste, and fosters a greener and more sustainable community.
